Who We Are & The Problems We Solve

Eco-Mars is a youth-led green startup committed to solving two pressing environmental challenges: deforestation and waste paper accumulation.

Every year, millions of wooden pencils are produced, leading to the cutting down of countless trees. Meanwhile, paper waste piles up in landfills, adding to pollution. Traditional plastic pens worsen the problem by contributing to non-biodegradable waste.

We at Eco-Mars believe that everyday tools like pencils and pens should restore the planet — not harm it. That’s why we design eco-friendly pencils, color pencils, and plantable paper pens made entirely from recycled paper. Each product carries seeds, turning the end of a pencil or pen’s life into the beginning of a new one — a plant, a flower, or even a tree.

Eco-Mars is more than just a stationery brand. We are building a movement of sustainability, where students, artists, professionals, and organizations can all contribute to climate action through simple, everyday choices.
